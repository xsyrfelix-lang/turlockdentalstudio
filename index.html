Our Story

A Father-Son Legacy of Precision

Dr. Ishaya was inspired to go into dentistry by his father, who has been the proud owner of Golden Valley Dental Lab for over 20 years.

When it comes to making sure restorations and prosthetics are absolutely perfect, there's nobody more in-sync than this father-son duo. That unique bond translates into every crown, implant, and smile we craft at Turlock Dental Studio.

Located inside The Tower Health & Wellness on Colorado Avenue, we've spent over two decades building relationships with families across Turlock and the Central Valley. We don't just treat teeth — we treat people.
